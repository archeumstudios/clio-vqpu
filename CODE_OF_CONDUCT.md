# Code of Conduct

Clio contributors are expected to collaborate respectfully, evaluate technical claims on evidence, welcome good-faith correction, and avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or disclosure of private information.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately through a verified Archeum Studios channel until a dedicated project address is published. Reports will be handled as confidentially and fairly as practical, with conflicts of interest disclosed and avoided.

This concise policy must be reviewed and expanded with verified enforcement contacts before the definitive public release.
